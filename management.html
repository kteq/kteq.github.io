The TERC board oversees KTEQ activities by meeting once a month with KTEQ staff. Visitors are welcome at TERC board meetings. Please notify the board if you would like to attend by sending an email to kteq@mines.sdsmt.edu .

The TERC board is made up of a combination of students, staff, and community members. Being on the TERC board is a fun, low-time committment activity that looks great on a resume. All students are encouraged to attend meetings as visitors. First- and second-year students are especially encouraged to join the board.
